About me

I'm a PhD student at Georgia Tech advised by Manos Antonakakis in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering.
My research focuses on operational solutions for problems in network security, including attacker detection, attacker attribution, and machine learning system robustness. I'm also the president of GreyHat, Georgia Tech's computer security club.
